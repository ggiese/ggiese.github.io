Biography

Mr. Giese absolutely loves math and computers. The only thing he like more than his beloved diciplines is guiding students through their own journey in the math and sciences. You are forutnate to have such a caring guide.

Sixteen years ago, Mr. Giese began teaching mathematics at West High School. He prepared for this by obtaining a degree from Bakersfield College, followed by a Batchlors Degree in Mathematics at Cal State Bakersfield. He earned his teaching credential at Point Loma Nazarene University, where he followed up with a Masters Degree in Administration. After all of this school, the most important lesson he learned was, he really appreciates his students who come through his door with an excitement for life, learning and growing.
